Our Aim

People do better when they understand what is going on with their bodies.
We are focused on empowering the public by:

  1. Giving every individual access to personable, knowledgable physicians willing to teach them about their health in ways they can understand
  2. Being a resource people can come back to when questions arise throughout their care
  3. Increasing everyone’s ability to have meaningful discussions with their doctors and be active participants in their healthcare

Our Story

We have been providing this service for our loved ones for over a decade. Whether explaining new medications, proposed surgeries, or life-long illnesses; breaking down the science to help people understand their health is a passion that fuels us. Recently we felt that people should not be required to have family or friends in medicine to enjoy these benefits. Collaboration with family and friends led to the development of Medsplained. We look forward to contributing to your healthcare story and show you the benefits of having a “doctor in the family.”
