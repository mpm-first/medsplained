How We Help

Medical Diagnosis

Let us help you understand the medical problems you have been diagnosed with over the years. Whether you were recently diagnosed with diabetes or your loved one was just told they have cancer, we can educate you and leave you feeling prepared to have meaningful discussions with doctors.

Procedures Or Surgery

Let us help you understand the procedure/surgery being recommended to you or a loved one. We will walk you through the procedure, expected recovery, and the other possible options that may or may not have been described. That way you can make informed decisions about your care.

Medications

Let us help you understand the drugs you or your loved one takes on a regular basis or has just been prescribed. We will help you understand what the medicine is supposed to do, it's side effects, and how it interacts with your other medications.
